Once upon a time...

There was a tree that bred endless life. Its branches extended high into the sky. In the year 596 the dark region of Ragnarock decided to attack the tree of life and take its energy. Unfortunately the mission was a success and new life stopped going on. The word was plunged into darkness, sadness and sorrow. After 10 yeass, in the year 606 a might group of hunters defeated Ragnarock. Since then, the tradition kept going on. We now call them Sakura Hunters.
